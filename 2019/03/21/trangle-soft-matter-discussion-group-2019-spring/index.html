Notes for 2019 Spring Triangle Soft Matter Discussion Group

标题：100 Years of Polymers: Not a Trivial Pursuit
主讲人：Timothy Lodge

Top Ten Current Challenges (for Polymer Science):

1. Theory of polymer crystallization

2. Theory of the glass transition

3. Sustainable routines to commodity polymers

4. Monomer sequence control

5. Better membranes for separations (water, gases, ions…)

6. Competitive (cost and performance) sustainable polymers

7. Polymers for personalized medicine

8. Fundamental understanding of ion-containing polymers

9. Robust polymer systems for plastic electronics

10. Scale-up and processing to retain/perfect nanostructure
